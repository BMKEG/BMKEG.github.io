The Biomedical Knowledge Engineering Group (BMKEG)

Open source code generated at BMKEG@ISI

We are an open source, biomedical informatics software development shop. We are funded from academic sources and have a strong commitment to develop open knowledge systems to assist and support biomedical scientists in their everyday work.

We gratefully acknowledge the support of ej-technologies, who provide us with an open-source license for their excellent install4j and JProfiler tools.
